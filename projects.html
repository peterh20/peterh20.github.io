Lego Robotic Etch-A-Sketch


Using Lego EV3 motors and sensors, I created a lego robot that can be programmed consistantly to write words and draw pictures. Two motors handled the X and Y directions, while a third motor was the pen up-and-down movement.

The picture shows a consistancy test, where I tried to measure how precise the robot could replicate drawing the same cubes.
